Our Journey

From 3 Founders to 100+ Happy Clients

Founded in 2023, Karmanya Infotech began with a bold vision: to make high-quality IT services accessible to Indian MSMEs and educational institutions. What many companies overlooked, we saw as an opportunity.

Starting with just 3 passionate founders, we built our expertise across web development, mobile apps, and custom software. We understood the Indian market's unique challenges: price sensitivity, cultural nuances, and the need for responsive, reliable support.

Today, we proudly serve 20+ colleges and 80+ businesses across India, with a team of 30+ talented professionals.
